Workflow Sequence
1. Loan Proposal Created  
2. Authority Check (≤ $100k aggregate)  
3. Staged Funding Plan Approved  
4. DocuSign Execution  
5. Loan Recorded in Loaner Ledger Repo

7. Milestone Review Every 30 Days  
8. Loan Closure + Archive
