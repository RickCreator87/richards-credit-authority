Governance rules

Policy

Human-readable governance layer that your automation, CI checks, and agreements should mirror exactly.

  • Tax separation Personal vs. org flows never co-mingle.
  • Dual approvals Richard + co-owner for any org-facing loan.
  • KYC requirement Verified identity before any disbursement.
  • Ledger sync No disbursement without a ledger entry.
  • Agreement binding No funds move without a signed agreement.
Mirror this page in docs/governance.md and keep it in sync with your automation.
