# Sponsorship Policy

This document outlines how sponsorship funds are used.

## Purpose
Sponsorships support the long‑term development of:
- GitDigital Products  
- OpenGrantStack  
- GitDigital Credit Agency  
- Solana‑aligned automation and governance tools  

## Funds are used for:
- Development time  
- Documentation  
- Infrastructure  
- Security and compliance tooling  
- Contributor support  

## Transparency
Major updates, milestones, and roadmap changes will be shared publicly.

Thank you for supporting sustainable open‑source development.
